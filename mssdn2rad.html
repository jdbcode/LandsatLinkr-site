MSSdn2rad

MSS images with scaled digital number units are converted to units of top-of-atmosphere radiance by the equation: 

Lλ = "gain" * QCAL + "bias"

As directed by USGS using parameters from LPGS L1T image metadata.

http://landsat.usgs.gov/how_is_radiance_calculated.php
